ABOUT BAE

Why should we save her?

Water is California's most precious and natural resource. How we manage it today will affect nearly every aspect of our future. When our state enjoys a year of abundant rain and our reservoirs are full, it is easy to think that our water supply is endless. But after three consecutive dry years, California is now experiencing a serious drought. Bae represents our water, and right now she needs your help! But fret not, you can save her. We've built you your own personal Baesic Water Saving Kit for your abode!

THE BAESIC KIT

A smart water-saving kit with wi-fi

Included in your kit will be a wifi-connected meter that can attach itself easily to your water meter, along with two other attachments for faucets or shower heads of your choice. Then, log into the mobile app and let us know you're ready to go! Sign in at the end of every week to check your progress and see what baedges you've earned!

The Tracking Devices

The Baesic Kit meter system has 3 different meters. You're given one general meter for tracking your water system throughout your daily actions and you're given 2 other meters to put on devices of your choice. They're connected to a mobile app for further information.

The Baedge System

The Baesic Kit baedge system has over 50 different baedges. It's a unique way to track your progress and achievements that you've accomplished while helping save Bae! With each baedge you earn you receive a certain amount of points that unlock rewards.

THE REWARDS

Goodies for each baedge you earn!

When you earn a baedge you also earn points! For each point tier you've reached we'll send you a physical reward based on the scale of what you've accomplished. Like any good bae, she'll want to get you gifts for how well you take care of her!
